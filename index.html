About Us

The Once project is dedicated to the development of free and opensource creative software on all common desktop platforms, mainly focusing on video editing the project got its name from the application "Once Video" which itself is a an acronym for "Open source Nonlinear compositing and editing"

Our Projects

  • Once_Video: The namesake of the project and a in development video editing suite focused on cross-platform support while being as powerfull and easy to use as possible. Check us out on GitHub
